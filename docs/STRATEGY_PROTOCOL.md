# Strategy Protocol: Operationalizing Empathy

## 🚥 The Traffic Light Framework (Costco Methodology)
| Status | Client Fluency | AI Role | Human Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🔴 **Red** | Low (1-4) | Data Support | **Maximum Empathy.** Direct human contact required. |
| 🟡 **Yellow**| Med (5-7) | Foundational Logic | **The Bridge.** Move client from Arithmetic to Algebra. |
| 🟢 **Green** | High (8-10)| Automation | **Oversight.** High-velocity execution. |

## 🗣️ The OPCS Framework
Agents are restricted to these four questioning types in Strategy Mode:
- **O**pen-ended: "How do you perceive the client's underlying fear?"
- **P**robing: "What evidence suggests this is a technical issue rather than a relational one?"
- **C**larifying: "When you say 'automation,' do you mean for the client or for your internal logs?"
- **S**ocratic: "If we automate this response, does it fulfill our promise of 'Human Heart'?"

## 📈 The Arithmetic to Algebra Transition
- **Arithmetic:** Establishing the foundation of trust and basic service.
- **Algebra:** Solving complex, multi-variable business problems for the client once the foundation is solid.
