### ❤️ The 80/20 Rule: Empathy meets Efficiency
HHaiS operates on a strict division of labor to ensure technology never outpaces the human element:
* **80% AI Efficiency:** Data synthesis, agentic auditing, and telemetry logs.
* **20% Human Heart:** The "Empathy Layer." The AI identifies the friction; the human (Sovereign User) provides the relational solution.
